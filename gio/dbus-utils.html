D-Bus Utilities

Various utility routines related to D-Bus.

GUID support: * g_dbus_is_guid() * g_dbus_generate_guid()

Name validation: * g_dbus_is_name() * g_dbus_is_unique_name() * g_dbus_is_member_name() * g_dbus_is_interface_name() * g_dbus_is_error_name()

Conversion between GVariant and GValue: * g_dbus_gvariant_to_gvalue() * g_dbus_gvalue_to_gvariant()

Path escaping: * g_dbus_escape_object_path_bytestring() * g_dbus_escape_object_path() * g_dbus_unescape_object_path()
