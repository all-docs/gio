Stops watching a name.

Note that there may still be D-Bus traffic to process (relating to watching and unwatching the name) in the current thread-default GMainContext after this function has returned. You should continue to iterate the GMainContext until the GDestroyNotify function passed to g_bus_watch_name() is called, in order to avoid memory leaks through callbacks queued on the GMainContext after itâ€™s stopped being iterated.
