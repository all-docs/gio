Description [src]

Asynchronously stops a drive.

When the operation is finished, callback will be called. You can then call g_drive_stop_finish() to obtain the result of the operation.

Available since: 2.22

This method completes asynchronously. Use g_drive_stop_finish() inside the GAsyncReadyCallback to obtain the result of the operation.
